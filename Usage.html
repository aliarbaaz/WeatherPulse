Usage Guide

1. Searching for a Location

To search for the weather information of a specific location, enter the name of the city or town in the search bar located at the top of the page. Press the "Search" button or hit Enter to retrieve the weather data for the entered location.


2. Viewing Weather Details

After performing a search, the main section of the page will display the current weather details for the searched location. This includes the temperature, humidity, wind speed, and other relevant information. The "Temperatures" card shows the current temperature, minimum temperature, and maximum temperature. The "Humidity Info" card displays the feels like temperature, humidity percentage, and wind degrees. The "Wind Info" card provides details about the wind speed, sunrise time, and sunset time.


3. Weather of Other Common Cities

Below the main weather details, a table is displayed showing the weather information for other common cities. It includes details such as cloud percentage, feels like temperature, humidity, maximum temperature, minimum temperature, sunrise time, sunset time, wind degrees, and wind speed.
